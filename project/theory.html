Color Theory

How does color theory make a design stand out better?

What is color theory?

Color theory is the study of how colors mix together to make visuals and affect human emotions. This important to keep in mind when making a design because you want your design to captivate the user.

Why is color theory important?

Color theory is important because it helps make your design look better with colors that work well together.
