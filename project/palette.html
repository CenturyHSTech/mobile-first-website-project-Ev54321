Color Palettes

What are color palettes and why are they important?

What are color palettes?

Color palettes are collections of colors(usually complimentary) used in a design

Why are color palettes important?

Color palettes are used to show the colors you are going to or have used in your design.

Your color palette should have colors that work and look good together.
